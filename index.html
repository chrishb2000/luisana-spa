Our Premium Services

Acrylic Nails

Full set acrylic extensions with natural or sculpted form. Durable, elegant, and customized to your style.

  • Natural or extended length
  • Multiple shapes available
  • Premium acrylic products

Aprés Extensions

Natural-looking gel-x extensions that are lightweight and comfortable. Perfect for a glamorous yet natural look.

  • Lightweight formula
  • Natural appearance
  • Long-lasting wear

Dip Powder

Odorless powder application that creates strong, healthy nails with beautiful color that lasts up to 4 weeks.

  • No UV light required
  • Natural nail friendly
  • Chip-resistant formula
